the mural

Since the beginning of human history, murals have functioned as a medium to prove ideas and existence.

Now recognized as a form of art, “graffiti” began as an act of vandalization in the ’80s and ’90s, which itself started as a means of expressing one’s existence through “tagging.”

In recent years, Citys and those communities have used urban murals to reveal the neighborhood’s local character.

Makuhari City would like to follow this custom and manifest it as a non-existent collective consciousness through mural painting. At the same time, consider cultural creation and propose this as the city’s first art project.

As a virtual social city, I would like to use the construction site’s temporary enclosure as a medium instead of a permanent wall.

I want to carry out this project by following the process below.

  1. (Deliberation on the overall direction of the project)
  2. Artist nomination
  3. Selection of (3) artists
  4. (Deliberation phase)
  5. Submission of the first draft
  6. Vote on the draft (remixing ratio)
  7. (Remixing deliberation phase)
  8. Submission of the Second Draft
  9. (Final draft deliberation)
  10. Mural art decision
  11. Production
  12. Exhibition
  13. Demolition
  14. Split ownership and operation by the city as NFT

Elements in parenthesis will be time dedicated to deliberation through the city communication channel.
