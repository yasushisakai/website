1.4 Ambiguity

New technologies have always created the expectation that they will fix social issues as well.

Internet itself had big promises that it will democratise things through reducing the cost of communication. Did it so? Don’t we see people weaponizing the internet to voice out what they believe in?

If freedom is realized through possession - the more possession, the more freedom - the freedom itself is endangered, as we can see today due to an increasingly overstressed biosphere.
