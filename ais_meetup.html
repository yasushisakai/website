5 How does it work? 2

  1. You list your asset (image, sound, tweet…) into the blockchain, with the edition number and price.

    \vspace{18pt}

  2. People collects the asset by paying that amount of crypto currency to the issuer’s address. The issuer writes down to the blockchain that that this asset belongs to the purchaser’s address.

    \vspace{18pt}

  3. People can swap ownership of that asset in a different amount as the reputation of that asset changes. Usually with some kick back to the original issuer.
